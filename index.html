Ditto

Edit a photo or generate one with AI and it lands in your library stripped of its date and location — nowhere near the original. Ditto copies them back across, so the new shot sits right where it belongs.

📱 iPhone 📱 iPad 💻 Mac 🔒 Privacy First
👆

Copy with a tap — or a drag

Copy the capture date, location and the rest from the original photo onto your edit in a couple of taps. Or simply drag the original onto the edited one to send it across.

🔁

Built for batches

Made a whole set of edits, or several AI variations from one shot? Lock in the original's metadata once and paste it onto each. Reverse the direction any time you need to copy the other way.

🗂️

Everything back in its place

Instead of scattering to random corners of your library, your edits settle right beside the photos they came from — sorted by the moment they were actually taken.
